# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students)
# General Safety Tips
In today’s college life, being online is almost constant—whether it’s for classes, social media, or quick UPI payments. But staying safe online is just as important as staying safe offline. Simple habits can make a big difference. Use strong, different passwords for your accounts and turn on two-factor authentication whenever possible. Think twice before clicking on unknown links or messages, even if they look genuine. Avoid sharing personal details like your phone number or address publicly. When using public Wi-Fi, be careful, especially while logging into banking apps. For financial safety, never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, and always double-check payment requests before approving them. If something feels suspicious, trust your instinct. In case of any cyber fraud, quickly report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call 1930 for immediate help.
